## About
Sunrise Electronics is an online electronics store based in Bengaluru, India.

## Support hours
9 AM to 9 PM IST, all 7 days.

## Shipping
Free across India on orders above ₹499. Standard delivery 3–5 business days.

## Returns
7-day return policy on unopened items. Refunds processed in 5–7 business days.

## Warranty
Manufacturer warranty applies; we help facilitate claims.

## Payment
UPI, cards, net banking, and cash on delivery (orders under ₹10,000).

## Common questions
- **Do you deliver to my area?** We deliver across India; the pincode is confirmed at checkout.
- **How do I track my order?** Share your order ID (format ORD-XXXX) and the agent can check the status.
- **How do I return an item?** Unopened items can be returned within 7 days; the agent can take your details and raise a request.
